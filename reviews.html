Hear what people are saying about Tufts newest dining option!
★★★★☆
John Kelley
Easily my favorite dining option on campus! I went last Saturday for the grand opening and everything from the food to the ambiance was stunning! I ordered the Mushroom Carbonara with a side of linguini. The Carbonara was perfectly cooked with just a bit of char on the top... delicious. The linguini was a bit al dente, but the pasta sauce was to die for! When I was there, the tufts Italian band was playing, and their music added to the experience. I highly recommend everyone check out this brand new spot for campus food.
★★★★★
Paul Johnson
The best thing to being added to Tufts Campus in the last 20 years!! I was excited about the new Cummings Center, but when I found out it would be the home of a brand new Italian Restaurant I couldn't believe my eyes. I was first in line on opening day and I was extremely impressed with every aspect of this fantastic new dining location. I ordered the Risotto Alla Milanese with a side of spinach and was blown away by the quality of this meal. After eating at Dewick for the past month this was a breath of fresh air. Everything was cooked to perfection, and after my meal, I was the first to hit the dance floor to jam out to the Tufts Italian Band. Everyone needs to check this place out ASAP!
★★☆☆☆
Juan Carlos
While the food is more above average for the tufts dining services, I could not justify giving this restaurant more than two stars due to the underwhelming atmosphere and limited menu. Before I even ordered, the band was playing so loud I could barely hear the person next to me! When I finally was able to get a table, the menu was tiny. I understand this is a new dining option, however, they must expand their selection of dishes, or else I don't think this location will last long. That said, I ordered the Pasta Con Pomodoro E Basilico with apple juice, and both were exquisite. I will be giving Jumbo Italiano another chance very soon.
Opening Hours: Mon - Fri: 11am -10pm Sat - Sun: 1pm - 12pm
