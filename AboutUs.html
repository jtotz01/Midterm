Our Mission

"To Provide Tufts Students with Authentic Italian Food"

About

A restaurant is a place designed to provide dining spaces and social environments for people to enjoy. Students at Tufts are seeking sit-down restaurant experience that they currently can't get on campus. A restaurant where Italian food is served and memories are made, Jumbo Italia brings a taste of Italy to Cummings Center.
