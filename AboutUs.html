Our Team

About Us

Willie, Director

Willie joins Jumbo Italia from his previous role as Chief Restauranteur of France. He brings with him a breadth of experience in the restaurant industry. Willie hopes to make Jumbo Italia Tufts' premier dining location.

Sam, Head Chef

Sam brings his 30+ years of culinary experience from various restaurants across the US to Jumbo Italia. A highly decorated chef, he boasts 5-star ratings from every top critic agency. Sam's mission is to give back to his alma mater.

Andrew, Manager

Andrew was formerly manager of Ramsey Kitchen. After having creative differences with Gordon Ramsey, Andrew left and founded Jumbo Italia. He is excited to manifest his vision for an Italian restaurant at Tufts.

Jacob, Chief of Strategy

Jacob oversees day-to-day operations. He also leads the expansion team that is breaking ground at SMFA, Tufts Dental School, and Tufts Med School in 2023. Jacob is passionate about strategizing restaurant efforts and is excited about joining the Jumbo Italia team.
